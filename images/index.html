Our Story

More Than Tea —
It's a Promise

Milk & Honey Premium Tea was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a moment that feels like home. Nestled right here in Glendora, we set out to create a place where great tea meets genuine warmth.

We hand-select premium teas — from delicate Magnolia Jasmine to bold Red Oolong — and pair them with house-made toppings, fresh foam, and real ingredients. Whether you're grabbing a classic Earl Grey or building your dream boba, every cup is made with intention.

We're your neighbors. Your corner spot. The place you'll want to come back to — because we promise we'll make it worth it.
